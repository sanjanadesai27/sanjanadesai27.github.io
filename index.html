Meet RoadieSounds

One of my favourite projects that I've worked on to date. RoadieSounds is a simple single page application that takes your travel plans and music preferences, and turns them into playlists for you to enjoy along your journey, which you can save to your Spotify account. As an added bonus, it'll give you all the information and links you need to attend shows in each city you visit.
